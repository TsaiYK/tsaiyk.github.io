MEEN 357 Fall 2023 · Texas A&M University

Engineering Analysis for Mechanical Engineers

Undergraduate Course · Mechanical Engineering · Texas A&M University

This course provides a practical foundation for the use of numerical methods to solve engineering problems.

  • Topics: solving systems of linear equations, curve fitting, root finding, numerical integration, numerical differentiation, initial value problems, boundary value problems, eigenvalues, and optimization methods.
  • Project: Apply numerical methods and Python programming to model, simulate, and optimize an autonomous Mars rover capable of navigating challenging Martian terrain.
