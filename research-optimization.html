Research Area

Multi-Fidelity Learning and
Optimization under Uncertainty

We develop multi-fidelity active learning and Bayesian optimization methods that scale to system-level digital twins with inherent noise, enabling data-efficient and interpretable decision-making across large-scale engineering networks.

Overview

Complex engineering systems involve expensive simulations and noisy experiments. Evaluating designs at the highest fidelity is often prohibitively costly, while low-fidelity approximations may not be accurate enough to guide decisions. Multi-fidelity learning bridges this gap by intelligently combining information from models of varying cost and accuracy, achieving high-quality solutions with far fewer expensive evaluations.

Our research develops surrogate-assisted optimization frameworks that rigorously handle constraints, noise, and multiple competing objectives. We employ Gaussian process models, Bayesian optimization, and active learning strategies to allocate computational resources where they matter most — intelligently selecting which design points to evaluate and at what fidelity level.

These methods underpin the broader DISO Lab research portfolio, providing the optimization backbone for digital twin-enabled co-design and real-time decision-making under uncertainty.

Key Research Themes

Bayesian Optimization

We use Gaussian process surrogate models and acquisition functions to efficiently navigate high-dimensional design spaces, finding optimal solutions with far fewer objective evaluations than classical methods.

Multi-Objective & Parametric Optimization

We develop parametric multi-objective optimization methods that map design parameters to entire Pareto fronts, enabling engineers to understand tradeoffs and make informed decisions across a spectrum of objectives.

Constraint Handling under Uncertainty

We design surrogate-assisted constraint-handling techniques that accurately predict feasibility probability, ensuring constraint satisfaction even when evaluations are noisy or expensive.
